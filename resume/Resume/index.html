Bio

Clayton Winkle is a creative and ambitious beginner designer who is continuing to learn and hone his design skills. He is a creative thinker, always trying to go above and beyond. He is already familiar with multiple Adobe Creative Cloud programs. With a degree in Creative Writing he is familiar with Word and PowerPoint as well as creative story-telling. He has worked a number of years in the theme park and zoological industry and hopes to put his new skills to use within those fields. He is always ready to take on a new challenge that put his current skills to use or teaches him something new.
